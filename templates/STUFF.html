APRIL BREAK CANCELLED

Doug Ford has announced that APRIL BREAK will be cancelled!

"Kids don't need april break - online school is very easy and straightforward"

Doug Ford says that kids are "already coddled enough" and that they "need to wake up to the real world, in which there aren't as many breaks". Kids all over the province are dissapointed and mad. So are parents who need a break from work.
"We need these breaks! I need time to cool down. I'm very stressed," says a frustrated parent. "From science fair to normal assignments, school has been really difficult for us."
Doug Ford says that this decision is final, and he will not change it. "Easter is enough, if you don't think that then don't do anything with your life."
